Assignment: Design an original T-Shirt and print it on the Brother GT-361 printer

Process:This was just a simple T-Shirt that said Junior Varsity on the front. I got the Reserve logo online and then uploaded it to photoshop and added the text at the top. I converted the file to a .ar3 and printed it on the Brother GT-361 Printer by first ironing a blank white shirt and then letting the printer do the rest (and steaming it at the end again).

Assignment: Print something on a golf ball, phone case, or box top

Process: I chose the box top because it seemed the most usable, as I don't golf or use an iphone. I got a .jpg picture of the Patriots logo online and then put it on a usb and sized it in the Roland printing program. Finally, I used the machine's automatic sizing system to get the dimensions for the box, fit the media to those dimensions, and just watched it print.

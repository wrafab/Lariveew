Assignment: Describe what you've learned about soldering and the process to make your solder project

Learned

  1. Do not let the solder points touch

  2. Keep your head out of the smoke created by solder wire

  3. Use wet sponge to clean off solder iron after every few solders

  4. Always put solder iron in holder, NOT on table

  5. Do not touch the solder iron, even if you are dared to

Proccess: To make my solder project, the spinning LED wheel, I first organized the parts by color and type (i.e. blue LED, red and brown resistor, etc.), and then put the in their respective places in the PCB. I then used the solder iron and wire to solder each pin to the metal on the back of the PCB. After that, I cut the remaining wire on each pin so it wouldn't poke out, and finally I soldered on the battery pack and put a battery in the cartridge. When I push the power button, the LEDs go around in a circle, slow down, and eventually come to a stop on one.
